Interactive Data Visualization Final Portfolio

by David Leshinski

Welcome to my Interactive Data Visualization Final Portfolio! Below are two of my projects for the course along with a couple extra assignments I completed. Feel free to look around!

Top Ten Steam Games

An exploritory visualization project that allows readers to see the viewership on twitch of games that reached the top 10 in player count over a short time period.

Gaming Stigmas in Today's World

A narrative visualization project that takes readers through the landscape of gaming today and attempts to breakdown some of the stigmas that have surrounded gaming.
